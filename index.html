Our History

Historia Yetu

Founded in 2015, KMC FC has grown from a local community team to a competitive force in the Tanzanian Premier League. Our journey represents the passion and talent found in the Kinondoni municipality.

Ilianzishwa mwaka 2015, KMC FC imekua kutoka timu ya jamii hadi kuwa nguvu ya ushindani katika Ligi Kuu ya Tanzania. Safari yetu inawakilisha shauku na vipaji vinavyopatikana katika manispaa ya Kinondoni.

Mission & Vision

Dhamira na Ndoto

Mission: To develop professional football talent, promote sportsmanship, and represent Kinondoni with pride in national competitions.

Dhamira: Kukuza kipaji cha kitaaluma cha mpira wa miguu, kukuza uhusiano mwema wa michezo, na kuwakilisha Kinondoni kwa fahari katika mashindano ya kitaifa.

Vision: To become a leading football club in East Africa known for excellence, community engagement, and youth development.

Ndoto: Kuwa klabu ya mpira wa miguu inayoongoza Afrika Mashariki inayojulikana kwa ubora, ushiriki wa jamii, na maendeleo ya vijana.

Our Stadium

Uwanja Wetu

KMC FC plays home matches at the Kinondoni Municipal Stadium, a modern facility with capacity for 5,000 passionate fans. Our stadium is known for its vibrant atmosphere during match days.

KMC FC hucheza mechi za nyumbani katika Uwanja wa Manispaa wa Kinondoni, kituo cha kisasa chenye uwezo wa mashabiki 5,000 wenye shauku. Uwanja wetu unajulikana kwa mazingira ya uchangamfu wakati wa siku za mechi.
