Stadium Guide

Everything you need to know about visiting Kinondoni Municipal Stadium

Mwongozo wa Uwanja

Kila unachohitaji kujua kuhusu kutembelea Uwanja wa Manispaa wa Kinondoni

Getting There

The stadium is easily accessible by public transport from all parts of Dar es Salaam. Use daladala (minibus) routes heading to Kinondoni.

Kufika Huko

Uwanja unapatikana kwa urahisi kwa usafiri wa umma kutoka sehemu zote za Dar es Salaam. Tumia njia za daladala zinazoelekea Kinondoni.

Parking

Limited parking available at the stadium. We recommend using public transport or arriving early for parking spots.

Maegesho

Maegesho ya kikomo yanapatikana uwanjani. Tunapendekeza kutumia usafiri wa umma au kufika mapema kwa nafasi za maegesho.

Food & Drink

Multiple food and beverage stalls available inside the stadium. Outside food and drinks are not permitted.

Chakula na Vinywaji

Maduka mengi ya chakula na vinywaji yanapatikana ndani ya uwanja. Chakula na vinywaji vya nje haviruhusiwi.

Safety & Security

Security personnel present throughout the stadium. Report any concerns to nearest security officer or stadium staff.

Usalama

Watu wa usalama wako katika uwanja wote. Ripoti wasiwasi wowote kwa afisa wa usalama au wafanyikazi wa uwanja.
