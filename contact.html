Directions to Stadium

By Public Transport

Take any daladala (minibus) heading to Kinondoni. Ask for "Uwanja wa Manispaa" or "KMC Stadium."

By Car

From city center, take Ali Hassan Mwinyi Road to Kinondoni. Stadium is located near Kinondoni Municipal Council offices.

By Taxi/Boda-boda

Tell driver "Kinondoni Municipal Stadium" or "KMC FC Stadium." Most drivers know the location.

Parking Information

Limited parking available at the stadium. Additional parking available at nearby shopping centers and streets.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Maswali Yanayoulizwa Mara Kwa Mara

Majibu ya haraka kwa maswali ya kawaida

How can I buy match tickets?

Nawezaje kununua tiketi za mechi?

Tickets can be purchased online through our website, at the stadium ticket office, or through authorized ticket agents. Online tickets are available 7 days before each match.

Tiketi zinaweza kununuliwa mtandaoni kupitia tovuti yetu, katika ofisi ya tiketi ya uwanja, au kupitia wakala walioidhinishwa wa tiketi. Tiketi za mtandaoni zinapatikana siku 7 kabla ya kila mechi.

What are the stadium entry requirements?

Ni mahitaji gani ya kuingia uwanjani?

Valid match ticket, no prohibited items (weapons, alcohol, glass bottles), and adherence to stadium rules. Children under 3 enter free but must sit on adult's lap.

Tiketi halali ya mechi, hakuna vitu vikatazwa (silaha, pombe, chupa za glasi), na kuzingatia kanuni za uwanja. Watoto chini ya umri wa miaka 3 huingia bure lakini lazima wakae kwenye magoti ya mtu mzima.

How do I become a KMC FC member?

Nawezaje kuwa mwanachama wa KMC FC?

Visit our Fans page to fill out the membership form. Annual membership fee is TZS 50,000 with various benefits including priority ticket access and exclusive merchandise discounts.

Tembelea ukurasa wetu wa Mashabiki kujaza fomu ya uanachama. Ada ya mwaka ya uanachama ni TZS 50,000 na faida mbalimbali ikiwa ni pamoja na ufikiaji wa kipaumbele wa tiketi na punguzo la kipekee la bidhaa.

Can I bring food and drinks to the stadium?

Naweza kuleta chakula na vinywaji uwanjani?

Outside food and drinks are not permitted. The stadium has multiple food and beverage stalls offering a variety of options at reasonable prices.

Chakula na vinywaji vya nje haviruhusiwi. Uwanja una maduka mengi ya chakula na vinywaji yanayotoa chaguo mbalimbali kwa bei nafuu.

How can I apply for media accreditation?

Nawezaje kuomba usajili wa vyombo vya habari?

Media professionals should email media@kmcfc.co.tz with their credentials and coverage request at least 48 hours before the match.

Wataalamu wa vyombo vya habari wanapaswa kutuma barua pepe kwa media@kmcfc.co.tz na vibali vyao na ombi la ushirikiano angalau saa 48 kabla ya mechi.
