About KMC FC

Discover our story, mission, and vision for Tanzanian football

Kuhusu KMC FC

Gundua hadithi yetu, dhamira, na ndoto kwa mpira wa miguu Tanzania

Our History

Founded in 2015, KMC FC emerged from the passionate football community of Kinondoni Municipality. What began as a local community initiative has grown into a formidable force in the Tanzanian Premier League.

Over the years, we have nurtured local talent, providing a platform for young footballers to develop their skills and compete at the highest level. Our journey represents the hopes and aspirations of the Kinondoni community.

From our humble beginnings to our current position as a competitive Premier League team, KMC FC remains committed to its roots while striving for national and continental success.

Historia Yetu

Ilianzishwa mwaka 2015, KMC FC ilitokana na jamii yenye shauku ya mpira wa manispaa ya Kinondoni. Kile kilichokuwa mwanzo kama mpango wa jamii ndio kimekuwa nguvu katika Ligi Kuu ya Tanzania.

Kwa miaka mingi, tumekuwa tukilea vipaji vya wenyeji, tukitoa jukwaa kwa vijana wachezaji wa mpira kukuza ujuzi wao na kushindana katika ngazi ya juu zaidi. Safari yetu inawakilisha matumaini na matarajio ya jamii ya Kinondoni.

Kutoka mwanzo wetu hadi nafasi yetu ya sasa kama timu yenye ushindani katika Ligi Kuu, KMC FC bado imejikita kwenye mizizi yake huku ikijitahidi kwa mafanikio ya kitaifa na bara.

Our Mission

To develop professional football talent, promote sportsmanship, and represent Kinondoni Municipality with pride in national competitions. We aim to provide a pathway for local youth to achieve their football dreams.

Dhamira Yetu

Kukuza kipaji cha kitaaluma cha mpira wa miguu, kukuza uhusiano mwema wa michezo, na kuwakilisha Manispaa ya Kinondoni kwa fahari katika mashindano ya kitaifa. Tunakusudi kutoa njia kwa vijana wa wenyeji kufikia ndoto zao za mpira.

Our Vision

To become a leading football club in East Africa known for excellence, community engagement, and youth development. We envision a club that consistently competes for top honors while maintaining strong community ties.

Ndoto Yetu

Kuwa klabu ya mpira wa miguu inayoongoza Afrika Mashariki inayojulikana kwa ubora, ushiriki wa jamii, na maendeleo ya vijana. Tunatazama klabu ambayo inashindana kwa uthabiti kwa heshima za juu huku ikishikilia uhusiano ima na jamii.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Community, Passion, and Development. These core values guide everything we do, from training sessions to community outreach programs.

Thamani Zetu

Ubora, Uadilifu, Jamii, Shauku, na Maendeleo. Thamani hizi za msingi zinaongoza kila tunachofanya, kutoka kwenye mafunzo hadi programu za kufikia jamii.

Our Home: Kinondoni Municipal Stadium

The heart of KMC FC and our passionate fans

Nyumbani Kwetu: Uwanja wa Manispaa wa Kinondoni

Moyo wa KMC FC na mashabiki wetu wenye shauku

Modern Football Facility

Our home stadium, located in the heart of Kinondoni, is a modern football facility with a capacity of 5,000 passionate fans. The stadium features:

  • Professional grass playing surface
  • Modern floodlighting for night matches
  • Comfortable seating for fans
  • State-of-the-art dressing rooms
  • Media and press facilities
  • VIP hospitality areas

The stadium creates an electric atmosphere on match days, making it one of the most intimidating venues for visiting teams in the Tanzanian Premier League.

Kituo cha Mpira cha Kisasa

Uwanja wetu wa nyumbani, uliopo katika kiini cha Kinondoni, ni kituo cha kisasa cha mpira wenye uwezo wa mashabiki 5,000 wenye shauku. Uwanja una:

  • Uwanja wa kitaaluma wa nyasi
  • Taa za kisasa za mechi za usiku
  • Viti vizuri vya kukaa kwa mashabiki
  • Vyakula vya kisasa vya kubadilishia nguo
  • Vifaa vya vyombo vya habari
  • Maeneo ya karamu ya Wageni Maalum

Uwanja unajenga mazingira ya umeme siku za mechi, na kuufanya kuwa moja ya maeneo yenye kutisha kwa timu za wageni katika Ligi Kuu ya Tanzania.
